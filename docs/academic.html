ACADEMIC HONESTY VS. DISHONESTY:     

Hi Class - please read the attached Full Sail University Academic Honesty Policy document (attached in PDF format). 

As you know, each student is required to follow the Full Sail University policy regarding academic honesty.  This means that ALL work that you personally submit is expected to be the result of your individual thoughts, research, and self-expression unless the assignment specifically states “group project.”  

Each month you have the opportunity to learn and grow. Your goal is to succeed and do your best. As you work on course assignments it is helpful to keep in mind our Academic Honesty guidelines. This will ensure and enable you to maintain the highest level of confidence in the integrity of your work. Please take a moment to download and review this document.

 

 

Any act of academic dishonesty will be handled in accordance with AcademicHonestyDocument.pdf.
